PlainConsent vs Cookiebot

An honest look at the alternative.

Cookiebot is the incumbent, and a capable one. PlainConsent is the small, flat-priced alternative. Here is the honest difference, including where Cookiebot is the better choice.

The core difference: how you are priced

Cookiebot Core is priced per domain, by the number of subpages: a free tier up to 50 subpages on one domain, then paid plans from roughly €7 to €90 per month per domain as your site grows. Several things (custom branding, geotargeting, 47+ languages, CSV consent exports, extra domains) sit in the higher or paid tiers.

PlainConsent has one flat price per site, and every plan includes every feature. Your site growing does not move you up a pricing ladder, and nothing is greyed out.

Where Cookiebot is the better fit

We would rather you pick the right tool than the wrong one. Choose Cookiebot if you need any of these:

  • Automatic cookie scanning and classification. Cookiebot crawls your site and categorises trackers for you. PlainConsent does not; you configure your categories.
  • The IAB TCF framework for programmatic advertising.
  • Very broad language coverage (47+ languages) out of the box.
  • A large enterprise setup with many domains and team management already built around Cookiebot.

Where PlainConsent wins

  • Predictable, flat pricing. One price per site, no per-subpage ladder, and a lowest-price guarantee.
  • Every feature included, so no greyed-out fields or upsell banners.
  • Zero third-party calls and published source, so you can verify exactly what runs in your visitors' browsers, or self-host it.
  • Live in minutes, with no login needed to manage your banner.
